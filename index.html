Information Architecture Gateway

Information architects have been working together to develop the Information Architecture Gateway, an online learning and collaboration gateway, utilizing the power of one of the largest international and virtual supercomputer networks in the world. The Information Architecture Gateway will also act as a portal that helps to connect, search, find, and use tools for information architecture (IA), libraries, associations, universities, researchers, individuals, etc. from various sectors.

What We Do

The gateway’s IA Toolkit will assist software developers to update their software for better usability, interfaces, interactions, metrics, and IA deliverables.


Our IA Gateway Resource Library contains a repository of:


  • Wireframe templates

  • Short-code patterns (for WordPress templates)

  • Persona/profile templates that include findability fields

  • Reference materials

  • Guides

  • And other useful materials

Information architects in both academia and industry are also working together to develop an online learning and collaboration gateway.

Who We Are

Our IA Gateway team consists of information architects with additional areas of expertise. Team members also specialize in usability, user experience (UX), library and information sciences (LIS), linguistics, search/retrieval, software development, interaction design (IxD), and human/computer interfaces (HCI).


Due to our team’s wide range of experience, education, and expertise, we will be adding many items to our IA Gateway:


  • History of IA

  • IA Research

  • IA Methodologies

  • IA Visualizations
  • IA Simulations

  • Training

  • Ontologies

  • IA Taxonomies
  • IA glossaries

  • IA translations

  • IA tools

  • Other IA artifacts



Meet Our Team


Bev Corwin, Co-Founder
Technology Director

Bev Corwin is Director of Technology at DDC, a Consultant, and Advisor. Bev is a co-Founder of the Information Architecture Institute (IAI), and Enso Center for International Arts. Bev advises and consults for non-profit and for-profit organizations.

Shari Thurow, Co-Founder
Search Director

Shari’s doctoral work is in Library & Information Sciences (LIS) with an emphasis on Human and Computer Interfaces (HCI), including information retrieval (IR), search usability, interaction design, & searcher experience.

Noreen Whysel, Co-Founder
Research Director

Noreen is a NYC-based IA/UX Researcher. She was the Operations Manager at the Information Architecture Institute for 10 years & held a similar role at the OWASP Foundation. She has a BA in Psychology from Columbia & a MSLIS from Pratt Institute.
